# 🌟 Welcome to Dante's AI Assistant!

This bot is here to help us all take care of Dante. It holds all the important information about his food, his school, his schedule, and how he likes to play and learn. Think of it as a shared notebook that can talk back to you!

---

## 🚀 Part 1: Getting Started (Onboarding)

When you first start using the bot, it will take you through "Onboarding." This is just a fancy way of saying "let's make sure we all agree on the rules."

1.  **Read the Messages:** The bot will send you sections of Dante’s information (like his Menu or his Education plan).
2.  **Say "Done":** If you read a section and it looks good, just type **"done"** or **"approved"**. The bot will then show you the next section.
3.  **Ask for Changes:** If you see something that needs to be updated (for example, if a nap time has changed), just tell the bot! For example: *"Change his nap time to 2:00 PM."* The bot will update the file and show it to you again.

Once you have said "done" to all the sections, you are ready for daily use!

---

## 📱 Part 2: How to Use the Bot Daily

You can talk to the bot just like you talk to a friend on Telegram. You can **type messages** or send **voice notes**.

### 1. Ask Questions ❓
If you are unsure about something, just ask! The bot knows all the rules we’ve agreed on.
*   *"What time is Dante's nap today?"*
*   *"What did Dante eat for breakfast?"*
*   *"What activities are planned for today?"*
*   *"Who is picking Dante up from school today?"*

### 2. Give Updates 📢
It’s very helpful if you tell the bot what is happening during the day. This helps Papa and others stay updated.
*   *"Dante ate all his boiled eggs for breakfast!"*
*   *"He is feeling a bit tired today."*
*   *"We are going for a walk in the park now."*

### 3. Change the Rules ✏️
If you want to permanently change a rule or a piece of information, just ask the bot to update it.
*   *"Update the menu: Dante now likes mangoes."*
*   *"Change the school pickup time to 3:30 PM."*

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for Success

*   **Use Voice Notes:** If you are busy with Dante, just press the microphone button and talk. The bot understands you!
*   **Talk Normally:** You don't need to use "computer talk." Just speak naturally.
*   **Shared Knowledge:** Remember, what you tell the bot helps everyone else who takes care of Dante too.

If you ever get stuck, just ask the bot: *"How do I use you?"* and it will help you out!
