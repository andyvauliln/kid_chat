## 1. Role & responsibilities
- **Primary role:** main daily caregiver; implement routines consistently.
- **Key responsibility:** report changes early so rules/pages stay accurate.

## 2. Daily routines owned by nanny
- Morning routine (wake, bath, breakfast)
- School prep + lunchbox
- After-school activities (varied)
- Dinner + bedtime routine support

## 3. Behavior management (day-to-day)
- Use calm, consistent boundaries.
- If escalation happens: always do **repair** (hug, name feelings, reassure love).
- Track triggers (transition, “no”, overstimulation) and share patterns.

## 4. Education support (micro-sessions)
- Daily: 5–15 min focused learning (numbers/letters depending on plan).
- Keep it playful: toys, blocks, counting steps, roleplay.
- Stop before burnout; do another micro-session later.

## 5. Food support (implementation)
- Follow `Menu.md` “NEVER” and daily targets.
- Vegetables: tiny portions + mixing strategies; keep it positive (no fights at table).
- Report what was eaten and what was refused.

## 6. Reporting standards (what/when/how)
- **Daily:** meals, mood, learning, notable incidents, sleep.
- **Immediately:** injuries, fever, vomiting/diarrhea, unusual behavior, safety incidents.
- Include: time, what happened, what you did, result.
