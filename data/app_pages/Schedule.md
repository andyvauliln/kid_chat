## 8. Schedule Overview

### 8.1 Monday - Friday Structure
- **07:30** — Wake up and bath routine
- **08:00** — Breakfast
- **08:30** — Go to school
- **13:30** — Lunch (food from home)
- **14:00 - 17:00/19:00** — After-school activities with Hirja (5 days) or Vanya (at least once per month, preferably 2 times)
  - Keep activities varied daily
  - Plan next day one day before and mention in report
  - Add new options as ideas arise
  - At least once per month: Vanya takes him somewhere just the two of them for 5 hours
  - Special events: Prison visits, charity visits, seeing how people in difficult situations live (organized by Vanya and Hirja, conversations prepared by Andrei)
- **19:00 - 21:00/22:00** — Home time
  - 4 days with Vanya (Monday/Tuesday/Thursday/Friday) — at least 2 days with active playing and full contact (not just watching him or watching movies)
  - 1 day with Andrei (Wednesday)
  - Clean cat toilet (responsibility)

### 8.2 Saturday Structure (Hirja)
- **07:30** — Wake up and bath routine (Flexible)
- **08:00** — Breakfast (Flexible)
- **08:30 - 19:00** — Go somewhere (Budget: 250,000 IDR)
  - Prepare list of places (Vanya and Andrei may add)
  - If new places discovered, inform everyone and add to list
- **19:00 - 21:00/22:00** — Home
  - Watch long-term cartoons/movies in English (1.5 hours) or TV series
    - Everyone to make list with at least 10 options with links
    - If problems finding content online, notify Andrei
    - Try to excite him about content even if initial resistance
    - Watch WITH him
    - Report any problems
    - It's okay to rewatch favorites, but show something new each week
  - Sometimes: Sleepover with Andrei if available

### 8.3 Sunday Structure (Andrei)
- **07:30** — Wake up and bath routine (Flexible)
- **08:00** — Breakfast (Flexible)
- **10:00 - 22:00** — Go somewhere, stay at home if not weather, or combination (Flexible)
  - Once per month: Vanya joins for at least 5 hours
    - Vanya should notify 1 day before (preferably earlier)
    - Rotate who chooses activity: Vanya chooses one day, Andrei next, etc.
  - Sometimes: Can stay sleepover with Andrei until school Monday
  - Swimming (weather permitting, maybe next month)
